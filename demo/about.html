About

It's fresher, better tasting, and helps support your local neighbors! Let's face it. Eating local fresh produce taste better because they spend less time in transit from the farm to you. Some local produce is harvested on the same day you purchase it. Who said farm-to-able wasn't the best way to enjoy a meal?

Buying locally grown produce, you are helping to support the local economy by paying local farmers for their hard work and putting more money into the community.
